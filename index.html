Alex Robertson is a Boulder-area visual artist, endurance athlete, and web engineer.

His work investigates systems, mapping, and phenomena.

He currently works at Muck Rack.

He previously worked at Bonusly.

He previously worked at Pitchfork and Condé Nast crafting rich longform content , editorial experiences, and building the future of content delivery.

He previously worked at Uptake.

He previously worked at Someoddpilot building projects for clients like Saucony, Chaco, The Clean Energy Trust, The James Dyson Foundation, The James Dyson Award, Society For Contemporary Art, and Thalia Hall.
